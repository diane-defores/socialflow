SocialFlow adapts to you

Whether you manage 2 accounts or 200, profile isolation is a game changer.

📱

Community managers

You manage social media for multiple brands and spend your day logging in and out. With SocialFlow, each brand gets its own profile with all sessions active at all times.

Real-world example

You manage the Acme brand and the Globex brand. "Acme" profile: logged into Acme's Twitter, Instagram and LinkedIn. "Globex" profile: logged into Globex's accounts. One click to switch between them. No logouts, no re-entering 2FA codes, no risk of posting to the wrong account.

💼

Freelancers

You juggle between your personal accounts and your clients'. Grayscale mode and the anti-distraction filter help you stay focused on work without falling into infinite scrolling.

Estimated time saved

No more 2FA on every account switch. No more logging out and back in. No more "I posted to the wrong account." On average, users report saving 30 to 45 minutes per day.

🏢

Agencies

Your teams manage dozens of client accounts. The built-in Kanban lets you plan content without leaving the app. Custom links bring your analytics tools and CMS right into the sidebar.

Typical workflow

"Client X" profile → check Instagram comments → reply on Twitter → schedule a post in the Kanban → switch to "Client Y" profile with one click. All in the same window.

🧑

Personal use

You want to separate your work and personal lives on social media, or simply reduce the time you spend scrolling. Grayscale mode makes networks less addictive, and the friends filter shows you only content from people you actually know.

Digital detox, the realistic version

You don't need to delete your accounts. Turn on grayscale, filter out sponsored content, and rediscover social media the way it should be: a way to stay in touch with the people you care about.
