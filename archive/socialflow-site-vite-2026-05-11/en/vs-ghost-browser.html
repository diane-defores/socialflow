SocialFlow vs Ghost Browser

Lightweight native app vs full Chromium browser. Two approaches to identity isolation for multi-account management.

At a glance

Ghost Browser

  • • Chromium-based browser with isolated identity tabs
  • • True cookie isolation per tab with proxy support
  • • Desktop only (Windows, macOS)
  • • Free (3 identities), then €21-46/mo

SocialFlow

  • • Native app purpose-built for social media
  • • WebView isolation, anti-distraction, Kanban
  • • Desktop + Android (~5 MB)
  • • €9/mo (€6/mo annual)

When to choose Ghost Browser

Ghost Browser is a capable multi-identity browser that works well as a general-purpose tool for anyone who needs isolated sessions.

  • ✓ You need multi-identity browsing beyond just social networks
  • ✓ You want full Chrome extension compatibility
  • ✓ You need per-tab proxy support for different geographies
  • ✓ You want to replace your main browser with a multi-identity one

When to choose SocialFlow

SocialFlow is built specifically for social media professionals who want isolation, focus tools, and a lightweight footprint.

  • ✓ You primarily manage social media accounts and want a purpose-built tool
  • ✓ You want anti-distraction tools (grayscale, friends-only filter)
  • ✓ You want a built-in Kanban board for content planning
  • ✓ You need mobile access (Android)
  • ✓ You prefer a ~5 MB native app over a full browser installation
  • ✓ You want an affordable flat price with no per-identity limits
