Pricing FAQ

Why isn't it completely free?

Sustainable development needs funding. The free tier is generous for personal use. Paid plans fund new features and support.

Can I try Pro before paying?

Yes. 14-day free trial, no credit card required. Explore all Pro features with zero commitment.

What payment methods do you accept?

Credit card and PayPal, via Stripe. Prices are displayed in euros (EUR).

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Monthly plans cancel immediately. Annual plans remain active until the end of the current billing period.

Is there a student or nonprofit discount?

Yes. 50% off Pro with a valid student ID or nonprofit certification.

Why is a lifetime deal available for SocialFlow but not for TubeFlow?

SocialFlow still runs mostly on your device: cookies and live sessions stay local, and the core product keeps working offline. We now use a small amount of cloud infrastructure to sync configuration between devices, but that cost stays low and predictable. TubeFlow relies on cloud AI transcription for every video, which makes a subscription model necessary.
