Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about SocialFlow.

General

What is SocialFlow?

SocialFlow is a desktop and mobile app that brings all your social networks together in one interface. Unlike Hootsuite or Buffer which rely on APIs, SocialFlow displays the actual websites in native WebViews, giving you access to 100% of each network's features.

How is this different from opening multiple browser tabs?

Profile isolation. In a browser, all tabs share the same cookies: you can't be logged into two Facebook accounts at the same time. With SocialFlow, each profile has its own isolated cookie storage. You can be logged into your personal Facebook AND your work Facebook simultaneously.

Is it legal?

Yes. SocialFlow is a specialized browser, like Brave or Opera. It loads websites normally. It doesn't use any unauthorized APIs, doesn't scrape any data, and doesn't bypass any protections. It's the equivalent of having multiple browsers open at the same time, in a single app.

Installation and platforms

What platforms does it run on?

Windows, macOS, Linux (desktop) and Android (mobile). iOS is planned. Why no Chrome extension or web version?

Why is there no Chrome extension?

Chrome doesn't allow cookie isolation per tab. All tabs share the same cookie storage, and no extension can change this behavior. It's a fundamental technical limitation of Chrome. Detailed explanation

Is the app heavy?

No. SocialFlow weighs about 5 MB thanks to Tauri, which uses the WebView already installed on your system instead of bundling an entire browser (like Electron or Chromium-based apps do).

Security and privacy

Does SocialFlow have access to my passwords?

No. When you log into Facebook in SocialFlow, you're logging in directly on facebook.com inside a WebView. SocialFlow never sees your password. It's exactly the same as logging in through Chrome or Safari.

Where is my data stored?

Login cookies and live sessions stay on your device. If you create a SocialFlow account, we also sync useful configuration data through the cloud: profiles, SocialFlow accounts, custom links, friends filters, and key preferences. Social network cookies never leave your device; to move them to another one, use encrypted backup export.

Can social networks detect that I'm using SocialFlow?

SocialFlow uses the standard system WebView (the same one used by dozens of other apps). To social networks, it's indistinguishable from a regular browser. There's no "SocialFlow signature" in the requests.

Features

Can I add sites that aren't in the list?

Yes. The "Custom links" feature lets you add any URL to your sidebar. Each link benefits from the same per-profile isolation as the built-in social networks.

What's the point of grayscale mode?

The vivid colors of social networks are designed to grab your attention and keep you scrolling. Switching the display to grayscale makes content less visually stimulating, which naturally reduces time spent scrolling. It's a technique recommended by many digital wellbeing experts.

Can I post from SocialFlow?

Yes, since you're accessing the real websites. You post directly on Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, etc., just as if you were in your browser. No API limitations, no missing features.
