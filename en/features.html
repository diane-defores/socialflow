A full-featured app, not just a link aggregator.

Isolated profiles

Create as many profiles as you need: personal, work, client A, client B. Each profile has its own cookie and session storage, completely separate from the others.

  • ✓ Switch profiles with a single click
  • ✓ Simultaneous logins on the same network
  • ✓ Cookies saved and restored automatically
  • ✓ Name your profiles with a custom emoji

🌐

18+ social networks

All major networks built in natively. Each one loads in a dedicated WebView with full access to every feature of the site.

Anti-distraction tools

Take back control of your screen time. SocialFlow injects scripts directly into social network pages to customize your experience.

  • ✓ Grayscale mode — reduces the visual appeal of feeds
  • ✓ Friends-only filter — hides sponsored content and suggestions
  • ✓ Custom scripts — inject your own scripts into any network
  • ✓ First-render injection — filters are active before the page even displays
🧘
📋

Built-in Kanban

Organize your posts and content strategy with a Kanban board right inside the app. No more juggling between Trello and your social networks.

📊
🔗

Custom links

Add any website to your sidebar: analytics tools, CMS, email platform... Each link benefits from the same per-profile isolation as social networks.

Cloud sync that stays honest

Pick up your SocialFlow setup on any device without rebuilding everything from scratch. Profiles, accounts, filters, links, and key preferences follow your account in seconds.

  • ✓ Profiles, active profile, and hidden networks
  • ✓ SocialFlow accounts and active account per network
  • ✓ Custom links and friends-only filter
  • ✓ Theme, language, text zoom, and key preferences
  • • Login cookies and live sessions stay local. To move them, use encrypted backup export.
