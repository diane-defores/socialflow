Pricing FAQ

Why is it free?

SocialFlow is an open-source project. The code is public and contributions are welcome. We believe managing your social networks shouldn't require a monthly subscription.

Will there be a paid version?

We're considering premium features for teams (profile sharing, advanced analytics). But all current features will always remain free.

Is my data being sold?

No. SocialFlow collects zero data. Your cookies and sessions stay on your device. The code is open-source — you can verify for yourself.
