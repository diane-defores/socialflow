SocialGlowz is built for teams and creators who need separate social contexts. In a few minutes you can set up isolated profiles, keep account sessions separated, and reduce context switching across networks.

## Why Profile Isolation Matters

Without profile separation, personal, client, and team social accounts quickly mix together. Profile-aware sessions let you avoid accidental cross-posting and leaking cookies between contexts.

## Set Up Your First Profiles

### 1. Start with Base Profiles

Create at least three profiles: Personal, Work, and Clients. Add the social networks and custom links you use every day to each profile.

### 2. Load Networks Per Profile

Assign X, Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, Facebook, or other platforms directly in each profile sidebar. Keep only relevant accounts visible per context.

### 3. Test Isolation Boundaries

Open social networks in two profiles and verify sessions do not bleed across them. If you ever see another context, adjust network visibility and relaunch.

### 4. Add Workflow Links

Pin useful external tools (analytics, CRM, mail, booking pages) in the custom links area so execution stays inside the workspace.

### 5. Capture Reusable Routines

Use the Kanban board to turn recurring daily actions into a repeatable system. If your routine is stable, your team can operate the same way in every profile.

## Checklist: First 15 Minutes

- Create 3 base profiles
- Add social networks per profile
- Add at least 2 custom links per workflow
- Enable grayscale/friends-only filters where you need focus
- Verify local session isolation before login-sensitive actions

## Ready to Standardize Your Workflow

Once profiles are clean, every login, post, outreach, and research session becomes faster. You can switch contexts in seconds instead of rebuilding state from scratch.
